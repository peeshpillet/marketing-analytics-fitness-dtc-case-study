Marketing Analytics Case Study
Overview
A comprehensive marketing analytics case study analyzing three months of daily campaign performance data for a DTC fitness supplements brand running multichannel advertising across Facebook, Google, and TikTok.

The business was spending $1.6M monthly with an overall ROAS of 0.86, significantly below its 1.2 target. The goal was to identify what was driving underperformance and recommend where to cut spend and where to invest.
Tools Used

SQL (data cleaning and querying)
Microsoft Excel (descriptive statistics, ANOVA, Pearson correlation)
Microsoft Word (documentation and reporting)

What's Included

Part 1: Data quality assessment and cleaning documentation
Part 2: Performance analysis across platforms, regions, creative types, product categories, and audience segments including inferential statistics
Part 3: Strategic recommendations, A/B testing proposals, and executive summary

Key Findings

Search creative was the only format exceeding the ROAS target at 1.31
Northeast was the most efficient region despite not having the largest budget
Protein and Diet were the only product categories generating positive returns
Performance declined 70% over three months, from 1.52 to 0.45 ROAS

Note
Dataset and company details have been anonymized. All analysis and recommendations are my own work.
